The people behind OSCN

Organizing Team

Anita Eerland

Behavioural Science Institute (BSI), Radboud University

I work as an associate professor of Communication Science – and Open Science Officer – within the Behavioural Science Institute (BSI). I joined Radboud University and OSCN in 2021 after working at Utrecht University and founding the first Dutch Open Science Community there together with Loek Brinkman in 2018.

Maximiliano Cenci

Radboudumc University Medical Center

My name is Max and I am an assistant professor at the Department of Dentistry at the Radboudumc University Medical Center. My research focuses on decision-making in dentistry and promoting research integrity through Open Science principles.

Lieke Corbeek

Radboud University

I studied Medical Biology at Radboud University and am currently pursuing a Pre-Master's in Communication Science. Within OSCN I support social media, the website, newsletters and community events.

Tim Middeldorp

Radboud University

I joined OSCN in 2025 as communication advisor for the Open Science programme. Open Science appeals to me because openness and accountability can help address growing distrust towards science.

Karin Kastens

Max Planck Institute for Psycholinguistics

I am Head Librarian at the Max Planck Institute for Psycholinguistics. Supporting Open Access and Open Science is a key part of my work, and I enjoy helping researchers publish openly.
