The people behind OSCN

Organizing Team

Anita Eerland

Behavioural Science Institute (BSI), Radboud University

I work as an associate professor of Communication Science – and Open Science Officer – within the Behavioural Science Institute (BSI). I joined Radboud University and OSCN in 2021 after working at Utrecht University and co-founding the first Dutch Open Science Community in 2018. I am involved in OSC-NL and the International Network of Open Science and Scholarship Communities (INOSC), and I helped develop the Open Science Community Incubator Program. I enjoy sharing experiences about Open Science and community building, and outside work I like cooking, hiking, reading, and puzzles.

Maximiliano Cenci

Radboudumc University Medical Center

I am an assistant professor at the Department of Dentistry at Radboudumc. My research focuses on decision-making in dentistry and promoting research integrity. I am an enthusiast of Open Science practices and aim to foster transparency and better research practices. In my free time I enjoy cooking, biking with my kids, and spending time with friends.

Lieke Corbeek

Radboud University

I joined OSCN in 2025. I studied Medical Biology with a specialization in Science, Management and Innovation, and I am currently pursuing a Pre-Master’s in Communication Science. I support OSCN with social media, website, newsletters, and organizing events. In my free time I enjoy korfball and spending time with friends.

Tim Middeldorp

Radboud University

I joined OSCN in 2025 as communication advisor for the Open Science programme. I am concerned about growing distrust in science, and I believe openness and accountability can help address this. I have a background in Cultural and Media Studies and worked as a teacher. I enjoy city trips, meeting friends, and exploring playgrounds with my daughter.

Karin Kastens

Max Planck Institute for Psycholinguistics

I am Head Librarian at the Max Planck Institute for Psycholinguistics. Supporting Open Access and Open Science is a key part of my work. I help researchers publish openly and explore new open science formats. In my free time I enjoy hiking, travelling, and watching crime drama series.
