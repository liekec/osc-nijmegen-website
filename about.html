ABOUT OSCN

Open Science Community Nijmegen

The Open Science Community Nijmegen (OSCN) is a bottom-up network of researchers, students, and professionals who are committed to making research more transparent, reproducible, and collaborative. Guided by values of openness, fairness, diversity, and inclusivity, we aim to foster a more open research culture by connecting people and sharing knowledge.

The people behind OSCN

Organizing Team

OSCN is coordinated by a multidisciplinary team of researchers, students, and professional staff who are committed to advancing Open Science within and beyond Nijmegen. Together, they organize events, facilitate knowledge sharing, and support a more transparent and collaborative research culture.

Anita Eerland

Behavioural Science Institute (BSI), Radboud University

I work as an associate professor of Communication Science – and Open Science Officer – within the Behavioural Science Institute (BSI). I joined Radboud University and OSCN in 2021 after working at Utrecht University and founding the first Dutch Open Science Community there together with Loek Brinkman in 2018. I am a board member of OSC-NL and INOSC, and I co-developed the Open Science Community Incubator Program. I enjoy sharing my experiences with Open Science and community building. In my free time I like cooking, hiking, reading, and solving puzzles.
