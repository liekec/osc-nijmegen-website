About

We are the Open Science Community Nijmegen (OSCN), a community of researchers, students and professionals who are passionate about making Open Science the standard in scholarly activities. We connect people across research-performing institutes in Nijmegen and create opportunities to learn from each other, collaborate and contribute to a more open and inclusive research culture.

What we do

Community events, talks and networking opportunities.

Workshops

Practical sessions on Open Science tools and practices.

Community

What is Open Science?

Open Science is the practice of conducting and sharing research in ways that make it more transparent, reproducible, accessible and collaborative. It aims to improve both the quality of research and its impact on society.
